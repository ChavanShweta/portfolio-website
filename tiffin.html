Overview

Purpose: This tiffin app was thoughtfully created for the homemakers who like to sell their food and earn extra money for themselves by staying and working from home.

Role: UX/UI design

Tools: Sketch, InVision, Photoshop

Timeline: July 2019-2020

Competitive Analysis

By searching for similar kinds of apps, I came across one app called Ubereats, but this app is only for ordering and delivering food from the restaurant, whereas my app is about only home-cooked food. Also, you can order different types of cuisines, traditional, regional food from different chefs.

Problems and Goals

I have searched many apps but I couldn’t find any app on the home-cooked food. Many homemakers are excellent cooks and love to cook fresh food daily for others. My user will be one who wants to eat freshly home-cooked meal and the homemaker who wants to sell their home-cooked food for making money.

Main Highlights

For the designing purpose, I tried to keep this app’s UI layout as simple and as easy as I can for the user to use. There is no such age limit on this app. Any age group between 18 to 100 years old person can use this app.

User Flow

Next, I created user flows to demonstrate the steps that the user needs to follow while completing a task based on their specific needs and goals.
